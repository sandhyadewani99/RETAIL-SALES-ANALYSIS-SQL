## RETAIL SALES ANALYSIS USING SQL ##

 📌 Project Overview

This project analyses retail sales transaction data using SQL to uncover valuable business insights related to customer behaviour, product categories, sales performance, and purchasing trends.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate practical SQL skills by solving real-world business problems through data analysis.

---

 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains retail sales transactions with the following attributes:

- transaction_id
- sale_date
- sale_time
- customer_id
- gender
- age
- category
- quantity
- price_per_unit
- cogs
- total_sale

 📈 Business Problems Solved

1. Retrieved sales records for specific dates.
2. Identified Clothing category transactions with high quantities.
3. Calculated total sales for each product category.
4. Analysed customer age patterns in the Beauty category.
5. Identified high-value transactions above a sales threshold.
6. Compared transaction volumes by gender and category.
7. Analysed monthly sales performance and best-selling months.
8. Identified top customers based on total spending.
9. Measured unique customer counts across categories.
10. Evaluated sales distribution across Morning, Afternoon, and Evening shifts.

---

🔍 Key Insights

- Product categories contribute differently to overall revenue.
- Customer purchasing behaviour varies by age and gender.
- High-value transactions play an important role in total sales.
- A small number of customers contribute a significant share of revenue.
- Sales performance changes across different months.
- Customer activity varies throughout the day.
- Certain categories attract more unique customers than others.
- Sales trends provide opportunities for targeted business strategies.
